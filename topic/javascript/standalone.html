Below is a musical dice game attributed to Mozart (K6 516f). Click on measure numbers in the following grid to construct a score. To play the game, roll two dice at a time to select a specific row in each column of the grid based on the sum of the two dice. Do this sixteen times to generate a randomized score.

Listen to or print the score in VHV (click on play button in upper right corner, or menu: File→Save PDF).
